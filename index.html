how it works

  1. Sign Up – Just give us some simple info: your email, name, and username. You can also set a savings goal now (or later—totally up to you).
  2. Add a Recurring Expense – Start tracking at least one recurring expense, like rent, a car payment, or a subscription. Don’t worry, you can update this anytime.
  3. You’re All Set! – From here, you can add more expenses to see how they impact your savings goal or even track extra income to see how well you're doing. For now, everything is manual, but we’re learning and working towards making this an automated process. Be patient with us—we're newbies figuring it out as we go!
